Chicken Spaghetti

Description

This dish has a wonderful Southern snap and yet is smooth and hearty! A great way to use last night's leftover chicken!

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces of pasta
  • 2 cups chopped cooked chicken breast
  • 1 can or 10.75 ounces condensed cream of chicken soup
  • 1 can or 10 ounces diced tomatoes with green chile peppers
  • 8 ounces processed cheese, cubed
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
  2. Cook pasta in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, 4 to 5 minutes.
  3. Add chopped chicken, condensed soup, diced tomatoes with chile peppers, processed cheese, mushrooms, salt, and pepper.
  4. Cook and stir over low heat until cheese is melted and mixture is heated through, about 5 minutes.
